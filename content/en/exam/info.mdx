## Exam Format

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| Questions | **60 questions** (single/multiple choice) |
| Duration | **120 minutes** (2 hours) |
| Proctoring | **ProctorFree** online proctoring |
| Location | Remote online (home or office) |
| Language | **English** |

## Passing Criteria

- **Passing score**: 720 / 1000 (scaled score)
- This is a weighted score based on question difficulty, not a simple percentage
- Approximately 72%+ correct answers is the target, but varies by question difficulty

## Domain Weights

| Domain | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Agentic Architecture & Orchestration | 27% |
| Claude Code Configuration & Workflows | 20% |
| Prompt Engineering & Structured Output | 20% |
| Tool Design & MCP Integration | 18% |
| Context Management & Reliability | 15% |

## Cost

- **Exam fee**: **Free** for Partner Network members (first 5,000)
- Paid pricing expected after the initial free period
- Retake policy: Eligible for retake after a waiting period upon failure

## Registration Process

1. Join **Claude Partner Network** (free)
2. Register for the exam on the **Skilljar** platform
3. Receive exam access link via email
4. Take the exam at your preferred time via ProctorFree

## Result Notification

- **Timeline**: Approximately **2 business days** after exam completion
- **Content**: Pass/fail status + domain-level score breakdown
- **Digital badge**: Issued through Credly
- **LinkedIn integration**: Badge can be added to your LinkedIn profile

### Domain Score Analysis

The results report shows your strengths and weaknesses across all 5 domains. If you don't pass, focus on your weakest domains for your retake preparation.
