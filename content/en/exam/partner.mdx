## What is Claude Partner Network?

Claude Partner Network is Anthropic's official partner program. Members gain access to free CCA-F exam registration, learning materials, and dedicated partner support.

## Registration Process

### Individual Registration

1. Visit the Partner Network page on Anthropic's official site
2. Enter your email, name, and organization details
3. Agree to terms and complete registration
4. Receive Skilljar platform access
5. Register for the CCA-F exam

### Enterprise Registration

1. Company representative submits a partner application
2. Anthropic partner team reviews and approves
3. Enterprise partner account is created
4. Exam access distributed to eligible employees

## Free Exam Access

- **First 5,000** registrants receive free exam access
- Available to all Partner Network members (including individual signups)
- Paid pricing expected after free slots are exhausted
- No official announcement on when free slots will run out
