## Exam FAQ

### What is the CCA-F exam format?

60 multiple-choice questions in 120 minutes, taken online with ProctorFree proctoring. You can take it from home or your office. The exam is in English.

### What is the passing score?

720 out of 1000 on a scaled score basis. This is weighted by question difficulty, so it doesn't directly equal 72% correct answers.

### When do I receive my results?

Results are sent via email within approximately 2 business days. You'll receive pass/fail status along with a domain-level score breakdown.

### Can I retake the exam if I fail?

Yes. You're eligible for a retake after a waiting period. Use the domain-level analysis in your results report to focus on your weak areas.

### Is it open-book?

No. You cannot reference external materials, other screens, or notes during the exam. ProctorFree monitors your screen and webcam throughout.

### Can the exam scope change?

Yes. When Anthropic updates the Exam Guide PDF, the exam scope may change. CCAFup tracks and announces any changes.

## General FAQ

### Is CCAFup an official Anthropic site?

No. CCAFup is an **unofficial** community-driven information hub. It has no official affiliation with Anthropic. Exam registration and certification are handled through Anthropic's official channels.

### What study materials should I use?

Start with Anthropic's official Exam Guide PDF and Academy courses. Supplement with CCAFup's domain guides, scenario walkthroughs, and cheatsheet. Hands-on experience with Claude Code is highly recommended.

### How long should I study?

For experienced developers familiar with AI tools: 2-4 weeks. For those new to the Claude ecosystem: 8-12 weeks. See our Prep Guide for detailed roadmaps.
